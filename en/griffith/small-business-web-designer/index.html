Custom Code vs WordPress for Griffith Businesses

When analyzing local business websites, we consistently find WordPress sites scoring between 30-40/100 on Google's performance metrics. This isn't surprising - WordPress comes with significant baggage that impacts your business success. Here's why our custom-coded approach delivers better results:

Speed That Keeps Customers On Your Site

Studies show you lose 50% of your visitors when a website takes more than 3 seconds to load. Most WordPress sites take 5-8 seconds because they're loading unnecessary plugins, theme files, and database queries. Our hand-coded sites load in under 1 second because every line of code serves a purpose - there's zero bloat slowing things down.

True Mobile-First Development

WordPress themes claim to be "mobile responsive," but they're actually desktop sites squeezed down to mobile size. This backward approach hurts performance and rankings. We build for mobile devices first, then expand to larger screens, ensuring perfect performance on all devices - exactly how Google prefers it.

Superior Security Without Updates

WordPress sites are constantly targeted by hackers because of their many vulnerabilities. Every plugin and theme adds another potential entry point for attacks. Site owners must constantly update everything or risk being hacked. Our static HTML sites have no databases or plugins to exploit - they're virtually impossible to hack and never need security updates.

Clean Code Google Loves

WordPress generates messy, bloated code that confuses search engines. Picture trying to count spaghetti noodles all tangled together - that's how Google sees WordPress code. Our hand-coded sites are organized and efficient, making it easy for Google to understand and index your content, leading to better rankings.

No Monthly Plugin Costs

Want to add a feature to WordPress? There's a plugin for that - usually with a monthly fee. These costs add up quickly. Our custom-coded sites include all functionality in the base price, with no extra plugin costs or surprise fees.

Built for Conversions

WordPress themes force you to fit your content into their pre-built layouts. Our custom approach means every element is strategically placed to guide visitors toward contacting you. We build conversion funnels directly into the site architecture, turning more visitors into customers.

Complete Design Freedom

Page builders and WordPress themes limit what's possible with your design. Want something unique? You'll hit their limitations quickly. With custom code, we can create exactly what your business needs - no compromises, no limitations, no settling for "close enough."

Long-Term Performance

WordPress sites slow down over time as plugins conflict, updates break features, and databases grow bloated. Our static sites maintain their blazing speed year after year with zero maintenance required. They're built to perform for the long haul.
